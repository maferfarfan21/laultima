Leo Alalú

He has studied Mime, Circus Techniques in Poland, Commedia del Arte in Bologna (Italy), Katakhali Dance Theater in Kerala (India), Meditation Techniques and Ayurvedic Massage (India). He is an indefatigable traveler, he has performed in comedy shows, in the most important festivals in Europe (Belgium, Holland, Italy, Spain, France, England, etc.), Latin America, India and Japan.

In his extensive artistic career, he has organized creativity and humor workshops, coordinated and advised various humor festivals in Europe and has also given workshops and seminars.

"My passion is to inspire people who are not satisfied with what is comfortable and normal in their lives, and help them recover their vital force. I accompany them to connect with their natural creativity and their most authentic side that will drive them to transform each area of their lives and fill them with intensity, freedom, passion and the desire to pursue their dreams"

About the workshop

This workshop offers innovative tools to develop creativity and humor.

In an easy and accessible way for everyone: inhabiting one's own body and flowing with it, working in a group, sharpening the sensitivity of our senses, learning new techniques and tools to create, learning that creativity is possible for everyone, that it is not heritage from anyone, and that there are ways to make communication easier and more enriching, ingredients that you can take into your personal and professional life.
